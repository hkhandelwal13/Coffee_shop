Cappuccino, is the perfectionists drink.

An even one-third balance of expresso, steamed milk and foam. The go to drink to get while hanging out with friends. The foamy mustache will just add on to the laughs!

Hot Chocolate, is the drink equivalent to a warm fuzzy hug. Winter breeze almost begs to be enjoyed with this rich chocolate filled warm drink. Don’t shy away from a few marshmallows and sip on.

Caffe Latte, the coffeehouse classic.

It is simply one or two shots of expresso with sweetened milk and a thin layer of foam.

If you don’t drink dairy milk you can always swap it with any of the vegan alternatives.
